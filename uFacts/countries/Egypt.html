Egypt

Africa

Water Status: Caution

Egypt does has clean water, but many of its citizens don't have proper access.
If you go to Egypt as a tourist you won't have to worry, but water can be an issue for many... only half the population is connected to sanitary sewers. Partly due to the low sanitation, 50,000 children die yearly from diarrhea.


Where To Find Water

Generally hotel you stay at should have access to clean water, as well as most tourist sites. You won't have trouble unless you venture to more impoverished areas, or into the desert.

Notable Attractions

Egypt is of course known for its great pyramids and tombs, truly majestic sites. There are many sand dunes that are absolutely beautiful, although they can be deadly if you don't have a sufficient amount of water while travelling.
