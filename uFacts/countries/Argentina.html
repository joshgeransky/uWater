Argentina

South America

Water Status: Clean

Argentina has fairly easy access to clean water for the vast majority of people.
Around 97% of the population has access to clean water, although in more rural areas it may not be piped into homes. Only 45% of rural areas have piped water, as opposed to 83% of urban areas.


Where To Find Water

As a tourist you won't have any trouble finding clean water, whether at your hotel or basically any tourist desitination you visit. If you venture to more rural areas you might have to deal with pumps, wells, and outhouses.

Notable Attractions

Argentina has beautiful geographical sites, from waterfalls to glaciers. There are also various cities worth visiting for the architecture, such as Buenos Aires.
